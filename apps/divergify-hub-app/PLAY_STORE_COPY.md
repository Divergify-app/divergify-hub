## App name

Divergify

## Short description

For brains that zig when the world zags. Local-first planning and focus help.

## Full description

Most productivity apps assume one clean, linear way to get through the day. Divergify does not. It is a local-first planner built for brains that need faster capture, clearer starts, calmer re-entry, and support that does not turn into more noise.

This release focuses on the daily work: getting tasks out of your head, choosing what matters next, breaking down the stuck parts, and protecting your energy before the whole day turns into recovery mode.

Core features:

- Planner views for Today, Inbox, Upcoming, and Overdue work
- Calendar board with day/week scheduling and drag-and-drop rescheduling
- Brain Dump capture for getting messy thoughts out fast
- Magic Tasks for turning intimidating work into smaller next steps
- Focus sessions with built-in nudges and end-of-session debrief
- Habits tracking for tiny repeats and low-friction routines
- Sidekicks with different tones, from steady and grounding to direct and systems-focused
- Local-first storage, JSON export/import, and no automatic cloud sync in this release
- Tin Foil Hat mode to block optional cloud assist and keep supported flows local

Privacy:

- Tasks, habits, focus history, chat history, and settings stay on your device by default
- We do not sell your data or use ad trackers
- Optional assisted help is only triggered when you use it and can fall back to local behavior when unavailable
